Warranty and Return Policy

Welcome to Smart Cart! We are committed to providing quality products and services. Please review our warranty and return policies for mobile phones, accessories, and related items.

Warranty Policy

We stand by the quality of our products. The warranty terms for each product may vary by brand and product type. Please review the terms below:

1. Product Warranty Coverage

  • Mobile Phones: All mobile phones come with a manufacturer’s warranty ranging from 6 to 24 months (depending on the brand).
  • Accessories: Accessories like chargers and earphones are covered under a limited warranty of 6 to 12 months.
  • Services: Service repairs and parts have a warranty of 3-6 months, depending on the repair type and parts used.

2. What Is Covered

  • Manufacturing defects.
  • Hardware malfunctions not caused by physical damage, mishandling, or liquid damage.
  • Issues covered by the manufacturer’s warranty during the specified warranty period.

3. What Is Not Covered

  • Physical damage from drops, falls, or mishandling.
  • Liquid damage or moisture exposure.
  • Damage from unauthorized repair, alteration, or modification.
  • Software issues not related to manufacturing defects.
  • Consumables like screen protectors, phone cases, etc.

4. Claiming Warranty

Follow these steps to make a warranty claim:

  1. Contact our support via smartcart12@gmail.com or call +91 000 000 0000.
  2. Provide proof of purchase (invoice or receipt).
  3. Submit the product along with original packaging (if possible).
  4. We will inspect the product and either repair or replace it per the manufacturer’s terms.

Note: Warranty services may take up to 14 business days depending on the issue and product availability.

Return Policy

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, please review our return policy:

1. Return Eligibility

  • Time Period: Returns must be requested within 7-14 days of purchase.
  • Condition: Products must be in original condition, with all packaging, accessories, and manuals included.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Used or damaged items not due to manufacturing defects.
  • Activated software or digital products.
  • Consumable items like screen protectors, tempered glass, etc., once opened.
  • Custom or special orders.

3. How to Initiate a Return

Contact us at smartcart12@gmail.com or call +91 000 000 0000 to start a return. Provide your invoice and a reason for the return. Approved returns should be shipped to our store (shipping fees are non-refundable unless the product is defective).

4. Refund Options

After inspecting the returned product, you may choose one of the following:

  • Replacement: A replacement product of the same or similar type, depending on availability.
  • Store Credit: Credit for future purchases.
  • Full Refund: Refund to the original payment method (5-10 business days processing).
